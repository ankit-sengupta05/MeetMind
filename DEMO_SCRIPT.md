# MeetMind Demo Run-of-Show

**Total Time:** 3:00  
**Presenter:** Ankit  

### Pre-Demo Setup
- Open Microsoft Teams on the desktop app.
- Have a simulated "Live Meeting" running in a Teams Tab.
- Open Microsoft Planner in a separate browser window.
- Ensure `npm run seed:demo` has been run.
- **Fallback:** Have screenshots of the Teams channel summary card and Planner sync open in a hidden PowerPoint, just in case the WiFi drops.

---

## MINUTE 1: The Problem & Hook (0:00 - 0:30)

*(Start on a slide showing a chaotic Slack thread and a blurred, messy notepad)*

**Hook:** "We’ve all been there. You leave a 45-minute meeting, and immediately the chaos starts. 'Wait, who is owning the Q3 pricing updates?' 'Did we actually finalize that API contract?'"

**The Problem:** "Information gets lost. Action items fall through the cracks. We spend hours trying to reconstruct what happened instead of actually doing the work."

**The Solution:** "Enter MeetMind. It’s an AI copilot that lives directly inside your Microsoft Teams meetings, automatically capturing context, extracting decisions, and assigning tasks in real time."

---

## MINUTE 2: Live Demo (0:30 - 2:00)

*(Switch screen share to the active Teams meeting window. Open the MeetMind Tab.)*

**Presenter:** "Here we are inside a live engineering sync. On the left, MeetMind is transcribing our conversation. But it's not just taking dictation—it's actively *understanding* what we're saying."

*(Simulate speaking / point to the live transcript where a tag appears)*

**Presenter:** "Right there. I just said I'd handle the database schema update by Friday. Instantly, MeetMind flags that as an Action Item, and you can see it populate right here in the sidebar. No one had to type anything."

*(Click the 'End & Process' button in the UI)*

**Presenter:** "When the meeting ends, MeetMind’s Post-Meeting Agent takes over. It synthesizes the entire transcript..."

*(Switch to the Teams Channel)*
**Presenter:** "...and posts this beautifully formatted Executive Summary card straight to our channel, highlighting all the key decisions we made."

*(Switch to Microsoft Planner)*
**Presenter:** "Even better, it syncs seamlessly. Look at Planner. Those action items extracted from the transcript are already here, assigned to the right people, with due dates set."

---

## MINUTE 3: The Memory Search (2:00 - 3:00)

*(Switch back to the MeetMind App, navigate to the 'Search' tab)*

**Presenter:** "But the real magic happens weeks later. MeetMind indexes every meeting you've ever had into a semantic vector database."

*(Type into the search bar: "What did we decide about the Q3 pricing model?")*

**Presenter:** "I can ask natural language questions. Look at this—it instantly surfaces the exact meeting from two weeks ago, highlights the snippet, and even jumps straight to the moment in the transcript where we agreed to the tiered pricing structure."

*(Pause for effect)*

**Closing:** "Every conversation, decision, and task your team has ever had is now searchable in seconds. MeetMind isn't just a transcription tool. It's an organizational brain that turns talk into action. Thank you."
